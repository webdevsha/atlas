Researchers often treat grant applications like lottery tickets—submitting to every fund (MOSTI, Cradle, MRANTI) without checking the fine print.

The Equity Trap: Missing the "51% Local Equity" requirement or having a messy cap table structure.
IP Ambiguity: Failing to prove ownership rights vs. university licensing agreements.
