The Positioning Logic

Ref: MIT Sloan's Deep Tech Strategy Playbook

1

Urgency (Problem)

The Question: What challenges are emerging or being amplified in your industry?

Critical: Must include Unit Economics. Don't just say "Satellites are expensive." Say "Launch costs are $10k/kg, killing margins for 80% of SMEs."
2

Momentum (Tech)

The Question: Which scientific advances are available to address this?

Unfair Advantage: Why YOU? Why NOW? "Our patent allows 90% efficiency where physics previously limited it to 40%."

Example Narrative: Starcloud

"Data centers consume 3% of global electricity (Problem/Urgency). Space offers infinite cooling and solar energy (Scientific Advance). Starcloud puts servers in orbit (Solution), reducing carbon footprint by 100% (Outcome)."
